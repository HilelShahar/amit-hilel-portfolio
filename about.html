About

Human behavior is my starting point.

I come from Social Psychology and customer experience research, so I naturally look for the "why" behind user behavior. I care about products that feel simple, useful, and emotionally clear — especially when the system underneath is complex.

Hi, I'm Amit

I'm a passionate UX Researcher & Product Designer with a Master's degree in Social Psychology and over 8 years of experience in customer experience research.

I specialize in crafting meaningful digital experiences through need-finding studies, usability testing, and satisfaction surveys. Beyond research, I also design and build intuitive websites aligned with user needs.

Other cool facts about me

Proudly mom to Romi and Ray — plus two mischievous dogs! When I'm not immersed in UX, you'll find me working out, hunting down food gems, or experimenting in my kitchen.
